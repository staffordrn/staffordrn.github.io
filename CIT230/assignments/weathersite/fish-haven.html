Outdoor Sports Are Big in Fish Haven

By Greta Gorley

The Fish Haven Trout, local men's soccer team are expected to do great things this season. With last season's big upset of the district favorites, the Soda Springs Springers, the Trout are poised to become the big fish in the pond. The Trout's coach, Phil Cortland, says "the team is coming off a big high. They're encouraged by the Springers game and have upped their practice time to keep their skills up. They want to go all the way this year." It may be an upstream battle for the Trout as the Springers are determined to redeem themselves from last seasons crushing defeat. "Its just made them more hungry for the win" says springers head coach Jim Gardner.
