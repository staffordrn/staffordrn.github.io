Picnic in the Park

By Bob Bobson

This fall marks the 10th annual Preston Picnic in the Park and Preson residents are gearing up for a big event. Since its founding, the Picnic in the Park event has brought more and more residents out to meet and greet and it has become a much anticipated event. Families will sometimes plan their family reunions around the Picnic in order to participate. This fall is anticipated to draw a larger than usual crowd due to the anniversary and also because this year's entertainment will be provided by local band Royal Platypi. Band members are excited to be able to participate in such a special town event. As usual, the Picnic will also include local food vendors.
