A New Temple In Richmond Virginia

The Richmond, Virginia temple was announced on 1 April 2018 by President Russell M. Nelson. This will be the first temple in the state of Virginia. Church members in Virginia are excited, especially in light of the closures of the two nearest temples: Washington DC and Raleigh NC.

The Richmond VA Temple

Plans for the 36,200 square foot temple at 10915 Staples Mill rd were submitted on 22 March 2019. An official rendering is still being developed, official reports indicate the Richmond Temple will be similar to the Pocatello Idaho temple, whose rendering is pictured here

The first temple in Virginia

The Richmond, Virginia temple will be the first temple in the state of Virginia, easing the travel burden for many church members who live there. Depending on traffic, travel from Richmond to the DC temple could take from three to as many as seven hours.
