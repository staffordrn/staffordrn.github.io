Welcome to The Temple Inn and Suites, your dedicated temple lodging choice. We offer many services and amenities to make your visit to the temple as stress free as possible. To best accomodate our guests we offer the following:

Services

  • Free access to changing rooms and restrooms for out of town visitors
  • A playroom and low-cost child care services for children of patrons
  • Long term stay accomodations for full-time temple missionaries
  • Comfortable temple history themed lobby and sitting area
  • An easily customized wedding reception hall with a simple menu and kitchen access
  • A family history center for family history searches and ordinance work
  • An affordable cafe with outdoor picnic areas
  • Family History consultation and family ordinance card service

Amenities

  • Free Breakfast
  • Exercise room
  • Indoor pool
  • WiFi high-speed internet and smart TVs
  • Free parking
  • Temple shuttle
